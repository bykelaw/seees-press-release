Press release
SEEES,3 August 2020
SEEES announces the end and of a great tenure and the commencement of a new one

We will like to appreciate every one who has contributed to make the 2018/2019 session a great one; we are so grateful for all your support as we know that we could not have made it through the session successfully without your help.

The end of the 2018/2019 came with a great challenge for everyone with the out break of the Covid 19 virus postponing the the resumption of the next session indefinitely . We express our sincere apologies for that and we would like to also remind you that this problem, just like many other problems we have encountered, came with some opportunities and we would advice you make conscious effort to maximize those opportunities.

The ending of the 2018/2019 session brought the tenure of the former SEEES president Ebunluwabi Alabi to an end. Her tenure ended successfully with SEEES experiencing progress both academically and socially. During her tenure SEEES had their Industrial Tour , in which students had the opportunity to visit the Coca Cola and the Gil Automation Company. The that was immediately followed by the Novelty Match , SPAC, Charity Drive , SEEES Dinner( La Chipsa de Royal) which brought the SEEES week to and end. Finally before the end of the session the we had the SEEES Picnic which was one of the best we have ever had. We express our deep gratitude for all the hard work she has put in to ensure that all these events was a success. We wish her success and greater highs in her next phase of life.

Memoir is about handing over your life to someone and saying, this is what I went through, this is who I am, and maybe you can learn something from it. In view of that SEEES would like to announce her new President Afariogun Precious who would be building up on the experience of our former president along with his own wealth of experience to take SEEES to a greater height. We encourage the whole of SEEES to give him their fully support so that this new session would be a greater one.

In conclusion I would like to implore all the SEEES members where every they are to embrace all the safety habit and do their best to kept safe from this Covid-19 virus. I believe this plage would soon come to an end and every would return to normal.
